A Note on Color

PMS is the print standard. Hex values are digital approximations for screen and Canva use. When accuracy matters for print, always reference the PMS number — not the hex. The primary brand color (#1F4A5E) is the dominant color used throughout thebikerandbaker.com for text, buttons, and navigation.

Voice & Tone

Be This

  • Adventurous — speak from the trail, the van, the summit
  • Wholesome — grounded in real ingredients and real living
  • Grateful — the Gratitude Granola spirit
  • Direct — short sentences work well
  • Warm — like a friend who wants you to eat better and live bigger
  • Confident — unafraid to have a point of view

Never This

  • Clinical or diet-focused — no "guilt-free," no calorie talk
  • Preachy — model the lifestyle, don't lecture
  • Corporate — no buzzword stacking
  • Overly polished — authentic beats perfect
  • Product-first — life and adventures come first

Always "The Biker & Baker" in copy — never drop "The." Handle: @thebikerandbaker

The logo itself doesn't include "The" — that's intentional for design. In all written copy, use the full name.

Content Pillars

Adventure Fuel

Mountain biking, van life, trails, open roads. Granola in the wild — not in a bowl on a clean countertop.

Rides & Recipes

Connect the cycling lifestyle to food. Recipes, ingredient spotlights, what we eat before and after a long ride.

Real Ingredients

Transparency about what's in the bag and why. Education tied to performance — never diet culture.

Gratitude

The spirit behind the brand. Slower, more reflective. Customer love, community, moments worth appreciating.

Find Us

Events, retailers, Shopify. Clear and direct — just inform, don't oversell.
